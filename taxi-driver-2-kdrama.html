The Taxi Driver 2 Kdrama series is one of the most popular and beloved Kdrama series of the year 2023. The show features a cast of talented actors, and viewers have been drawn to the story and character arcs of each character. It's a story of perseverance, friendship, and determination amid the challenges of life.

The Characters & Story

The cast of Taxi Driver 2 includes Lee Je Hoon, Kim Eui Sung, Park Hoon, Yoo Da In, Kim Seon Ho, and Jung Ji So. The series follows the lives of these taxi drivers as they struggle to make ends meet. Lee Je Hoon plays the main character of the show, Park Hoon, who works as a taxi driver to support his family and pay for his medical bills. Kim Eui Sung plays the role of Kim Seon Ho, a former military officer who has now become a taxi driver. Park Hoon is the driver who has to take care of his family and his medical bills, while Kim Seon Ho is a driver who is dealing with his own personal demons. The show covers a range of topics, from relationships to career goals and more.
